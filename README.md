# 🏆 HomeHarvest AI – Smart Urban Gardening & Local Produce Platform

## 🎯 HACKATHON ALIGNMENT
**Theme:** Social Relevance, Community, and Social Impact
**Addressed Problem Statement:** #6 - Community Resource Sharing & Support Network.

## 📄 ABSTRACT
HomeHarvest AI is a smart urban gardening and community produce platform designed for households with little to no farming experience. It addresses the core need for community resource sharing by enabling urban residents to grow their own food and share surplus produce with their neighbors. The platform provides personalized AI-guided recommendations, smart reminders, and multi-language support. In addition, it features a localized map-based marketplace, promoting sustainability, mutual support, and stronger communities.

## 💡 CORE IDEA (SIMPLE)
**For people who say:** “I want to grow plants but I don’t know how”

**This app:**
- guides them step-by-step
- helps them grow food at home
- connects them with nearby neighbors to share/sell produce

## 🔥 CORE FEATURES (FULL SCOPE FOR 4-PERSON TEAM)

### 🧠 1. AI Gardening Assistant (MAIN USP)
- **User says:** “I have a balcony with sunlight”
- **AI suggests:** what to grow (mint, tomato, spinach), how to start, how much water needed

### 🌱 2. What to Grow (Personalized Recommendations)
- **Input:** space (balcony / terrace / indoor), sunlight, location
- **Output:** best plants for YOU

### 📅 3. Sowing & Harvest Calendar
- **For beginners:** “Plant seeds today” | “Harvest in 25 days”
- Simple, visual guidance

### 🔔 4. Smart Reminders
- Water plants 💧
- Add fertilizer 🧴
- Harvest time 🌾
- *Makes it foolproof for beginners*

### 🧴 5. Fertilizer & Care Suggestions
- “Use organic compost once a week”
- “Avoid overwatering”
- *Simple, beginner-friendly advice*

### 🛒 6. Mini E-Commerce & Homegrown Produce Marketplace
- Users can buy seeds, pots, soil, and fertilizers.
- People can share, sell, or barter extra homegrown produce.
- **Example:** “Fresh spinach grown locally for ₹20 or trade”

### 🗺️ 7. Map Integration (VERY STRONG FEATURE)
- Shows: nearby sellers, local plant stores, community growers.
- **Example:** “Find resources near me”

### 💬 8. AI Chatbot
- **User asks:** “My plant leaves are yellow”
- **AI:** identifies issue, suggests solution

### 🌐 9. Local Language Support
- Malayalam / Hindi / English
- *Makes it usable for everyone in the community*

### 📰 10. Urban Gardening News & Tips
- Seasonal tips, trending plants, organic farming hacks

## 💼 LONG-TERM SUSTAINABILITY & REVENUE MODEL
To ensure the platform can be maintained long-term:
1. **Local Business Subscriptions:** Nurseries and gardening supply stores pay a small monthly fee to appear as "Verified Suppliers" on the neighborhood map.
2. **Promoted Community Listings:** Users can pay a micro-transaction to boost visibility for selling large batches of produce.
3. **In-App Affiliate Products:** Recommending specific soil/fertilizer brands and earning a small commission on sales.
